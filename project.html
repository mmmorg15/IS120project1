The Way of Kings by Brandon Sanderson is an epic fantasy adventure set in the storm-ravaged world of Roshar. It follows a cast of unforgettable characters, including a disgraced soldier-turned-slave, a scholar with secrets, and a nobleman struggling to do the right thing in a corrupt world. With magical swords, legendary knights, and a whole lot of storm-fueled chaos, this book kicks off The Stormlight Archive with action, intrigue, and Sanderson’s signature world-building. Expect big battles, bigger mysteries, and plenty of moments that will make you go, “Wait… what just happened?!
